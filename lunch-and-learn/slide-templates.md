## 🎭 Animation Notes

1. **Reveal Animations**:
   - Bullet points: Appear one by one
   - Comparisons: Side-by-side fade-in
   - Results: Typewriter effect

2. **Transitions**:
   - Demo slides: Smooth slide
   - Section changes: Fade through black
   - Examples: Zoom into detail

3. **Interactive Elements**:
   - Polls: Live results display
   - Timers: Countdown visible
   - QR codes: Pulse animation

4. **Color Coding**:
   - Prompts: Blue background
   - Results: Green background
   - Warnings: Red elements
   - Tips: Yellow highlights
